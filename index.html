At MentalPlay, we believe in the power of creativity and innovation to transform the way you interact with technology. Whether you're here to explore our latest products, stay updated with the latest news, or simply learn more about who we are, we have something for everyone.

Addressing the Global Mental Health Crisis

Mental health issues affect millions of people worldwide. The stigma and lack of awareness around mental health lead to suffering in silence. At MentalPlay, we are dedicated to changing this narrative by creating engaging educational tools that raise awareness and provide support.

Our Solution Beyond Gaming

While our games serve as an entry point for mental health education, our solution extends beyond the digital realm. We offer workshops, seminars, and community outreach programs designed to foster understanding and provide practical tools for managing mental health.
