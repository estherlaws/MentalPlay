MentalPlay. Who Are We?

Our Mission

We strive to evolve our products based on the feedback we receive from our consumers. In each game, there is a poll allowing those who play our games to provide us with feedback based on their experience to better ensure that future versions of the game meet our players expectations.

Our Values

MentalPlay allows our employees to feel as if their work has meaning. Our associates have a passion for educating people about an issue that all humankind will experience in some point of their life, as well as themselves as they perform their different roles in the production of our games. We also seek to bring inspiration into our customers' homes and lives that there is an answer to the mental battle they are facing and that there is someone who cares. We pride ourselves on compassion and empathy.

Our Future

MentalPlay thrives on defining our success upon our customers experiences with our products. If our purchasers are not happy with our products or their expectations have not been met, we have not succeeded as a company. MentalPlay is solely focused on making sure that our products bring yourself, your friends, and your family closer together, and being able to enjoy learning about mental health. TOGETHER.
